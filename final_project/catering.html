CATERING

Planning a graduation party or wedding reception? Let us cater your special event. We offer many services, including hall rental, food delivery, and serving.

Hall Rental

Come and rent Diabetty’s Hall of Delectable Delights. Our hall, has room for 100 guests. Whether it is for a few hours or the whole day, we can cater your event and make it one you’ll never forget.

Food Delivery

Looking for a healthy treaty that all your guests will love to eat? Look no further then Diabetty’s food delivery service. We bring the cool, and you get all the credit. We can deliver to nearly any location, just call us twenty minutes in advance, and a special delivery will be made right to your front door.

Serving

Need some extra help with serving your guests a delicious dessert? Our serving service does just that. If you want a nostalgic feel, we recommend hiring our ice cream truck service. Want something that is low key? We are happy to have our professional ice cream artists scoop up the best-looking ice cream you have ever seen.

Custom Flavors

Do you have a favorite flavor that is not listed? No problem! Contact us today about creating a custom flavor just for you. We do require a 1 gallon minimum for custom flavors.
